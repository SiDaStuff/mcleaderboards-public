Step 1: Link Your Minecraft Account

To participate in MC Leaderboards, we need to link your Minecraft username to your account. Enter your Minecraft username and select your region to begin the verification process.

Enter your exact Minecraft username (validated via Mojang API)
Select your primary gaming region for better matchmaking

Step 2: Verify Your Minecraft Account

To complete account linking, you need to verify ownership of your Minecraft account by joining one of our servers and running the verification command.

Active Servers

Join one of these servers where MCLeaderboardsAuth is installed and run the /link command with your verification code:

mc.sidastuff.com Main SMP Server
spectorsmp.pineserver.xyz Spectator SMP Server
Verification Timeout

You have 15 minutes to complete this verification. If it expires, you'll need to restart the linking process.
