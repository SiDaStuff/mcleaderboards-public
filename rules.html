###overall
# MC Leaderboards Rules

## General Conduct
- Be respectful to players and testers.
- No harassment, spam, or disruptive behavior.
- Follow tester instructions during active tests.

## Cheating and Restrictions
- Cheating, helping cheaters, or hiding cheating evidence can result in restrictions or permanent bans.
- Repeat misconduct after punishment may result in permanent removal.

### What counts as cheating?
- Account sharing in ranked fights.
- Rigged/faked fights or scores.
- Unfair advantages through disallowed mods, plugins, or commands.
- Any deliberate attempt to manipulate testing outcomes.

## Mod and Client Rules
### Allowed (examples)
- Cosmetic mods (capes, crosshairs, overlays).
- Performance mods that do not change gameplay.
- Personal HUD mods (armor HUD, potion HUD, saturation HUD).

### Disallowed
- Movement/reach/combat automation mods (macros, autoclickers, auto totem, etc).
- Mods that create major non-vanilla behavior differences.
- ESP/radar/free-look/minimap-style opponent tracking.
- Mods that reveal hidden opponent info (health, durability, saturation, etc).
- Modifications that intentionally cause abnormal latency or packet abuse.

## Queue and Matchmaking
- You may queue even if no tester is currently online.
- Matchmaking is based on gamemode + region compatibility.
- If a tester becomes available for your gamemode and region, queued players are matched automatically.
- If no tester is available for 5+ minutes, queue auto-removal may occur.

## Universal Match Rules
- No stalling (excessive running/camping/avoidance).
- Do not leave tests mid-session without valid reason.
- Do not attack obviously frozen/disconnected opponents unfairly.
- Do not spam players during tests.
- Use common sense and fair-play standards.

## First-To Targets
- Sword: FT6
- Axe: FT10
- Pot: FT5
- NethOP: FT3
- SMP: FT2
